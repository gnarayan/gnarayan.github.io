Biography

Gautham Narayan is an Associate Professor at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), USA, Deputy Director for Astrophysics at the NSF-Simons SkAI Institute, Deputy Director of the Center for Astrophysical Surveys (CAPS) at UIUC/NCSA, Spokesperson of the LSST Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC), co-PI of the Young Supernova Experiment (YSE), and PI of SCiMMA (Scalable Cyberinfrastructure to support Multi-Messenger Astrophysics).

His research spans observational cosmology, time-domain astrophysics, wide-field UV/optical/IR surveys, multi-messenger astrophysics, and the application of statistics, data science, and AI to astronomical datasets. He develops methods for real-time detection and classification of transients, Bayesian models for cosmological inference with Type Ia supernovae, and foundation AI models for astrophysical survey science in the era of the Vera C. Rubin Observatory and the Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST).
